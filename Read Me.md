**Business Growth Analysis: Forecast Repeat Order & Referral to Revenue** (English)

1. Project Overview  
   This analysis focuses on the company’s North Star Metrics:  
* Repeat Orders → an indicator of customer satisfaction and loyalty.  
* Referrals → primary source of leads for revenue growth.  
  This notebook presents data exploration, trend visualizations, and strategic recommendations to understand business growth patterns.  
2. Objectives  
* Identify growth and decline trends in Repeat Orders & Referrals.  
* Assess the effectiveness of lead conversion through to the closing stage.  
* Evaluate the contribution of service categories to revenue.  
* Provide strategic recommendations for customer retention and referral optimization.  
    
3.  Dataset  
* Source: Internal data (CSV, 997 rows, 17 columns).  
* Key variables: type\_of\_leads\_clean, status\_category, service\_category\_big, value\_fixed, year\_period.  
* Period: 2022–2026.  
    
4. Methodology  
* Data Cleaning → remove irrelevant columns, convert data types.  
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) → lead distribution, annual trends, status category.  
* Visualization → line charts & bar charts for growth trends, revenue, and status distribution.[View Dashboard Here](https://datastudio.google.com/reporting/6d156873-530f-43e5-8128-8cc563d4b9ba)  
* Insight Extraction → bottleneck analysis, dominant service categories, strategic recommendations.  
    
5.  Key Findings  
1. Repeat Orders  
* A sharp decline since 2024 (revenue down 72.87%).  
* Main bottleneck: 40% of leads stall at the “Send Proposal” stage.  
* The Immigration & Foreign Workers category remains a key revenue driver (IDR 974 million).  
2. Referrals  
* Consistent negative trend since 2022 (revenue down \-51% in 2024).  
* Similar bottleneck: 53.7% of leads stall at “Send Proposal.”  
* Dominant referrer: RT Referrals (Rp 678 million in revenue).  
3.  Overall  
* Repeat Orders & Referrals still contribute significantly to revenue compared to New Prospects/Organic.  
* However, both show a concerning downward trend. Forecasts also indicate a concerning downward trend.  
    
6. Strategic Recommendations  
* Customer Retention: Loyalty programs, progressive discounts, free consultations, dedicated relationship managers.  
* Proposal Optimization: Proactive follow-ups, strengthen value proposition, time-limited incentives.  
* Referral Management: Reward key referrers, identify “Unknown Referrers,” conduct referral quality surveys.  
* Service Focus: Maximize conversions in the Immigration & Foreign Workers, Family Law, and Corporate categories.  
7. Tech Stack  
* Python Libraries: pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn  
* Environment: Google Colab, Looker/Data Studio  
* Visualization: Line chart, bar chart, stacked bar chart  
* Dashboard : Data/Looker Studio

**Business Growth Analysis: Forecast Repeat Order & Referral to Revenue** (Indonesia)

1. Project Overview  
   Analisis ini berfokus pada North Star Metrics perusahaan:  
* Repeat Order → indikator kepuasan & loyalitas pelanggan.  
* Referral → sumber leads utama untuk pertumbuhan revenue.  
  Notebook ini menyajikan eksplorasi data, visualisasi tren, serta rekomendasi strategis untuk memahami pola pertumbuhan bisnis.  
    
2. Objectives  
* Mengidentifikasi tren pertumbuhan dan penurunan pada Repeat Order & Referral.  
* Menilai efektivitas konversi leads hingga tahap closing.  
* Mengevaluasi kontribusi kategori layanan terhadap revenue.  
* Memberikan rekomendasi strategis untuk retensi pelanggan dan optimalisasi referral.

3. Dataset  
* Sumber: Data internal (CSV, 997 baris, 17 kolom).  
* Variabel utama:type\_of\_leads\_clean, status\_category, service\_category\_big, value\_fixed, year\_period.  
* Periode: 2022–2026.  
    
4. Methodology  
* Data Cleaning→ drop kolom tidak relevan, ubah tipe data.  
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) → distribusi leads, tren tahunan, status category.  
* Visualization → line chart & bar chart untuk tren pertumbuhan, revenue, dan distribusi status. [View Dashboard Here](https://datastudio.google.com/reporting/6d156873-530f-43e5-8128-8cc563d4b9ba)  
* Insight Extraction → analisis bottleneck, kategori layanan dominan, rekomendasi strategis.  
    
5.  Key Findings  
1.  Repeat Order  
* Penurunan drastis sejak 2024 (revenue \-72.87%).  
* Bottleneck utama: 40% leads berhenti di tahap “Kirim Proposal”  
*  Kategori Imigrasi & TKA tetap menjadi pilar revenue (Rp 974 juta).

2.  Referral  
* Tren negatif konsisten sejak 2022 (revenue turun \-51% di 2024).  
* Bottleneck serupa: 53.7% leads tertahan di “Kirim Proposal”.  
* Referrer dominan: Referral RT(Rp 678 juta revenue).

3.   Overall  
* Repeat Order & Referral masih menyumbang revenue signifikan dibanding New Prospect/Organic.  
*  Namun, keduanya menunjukkan tren penurunan yang mengkhawatirkan.Forecasting juga menunjukan tren penurunan yang mengkhawatirakan.  
    
6. Strategic Recommendations  
* Retensi Pelanggan: Program loyalitas, diskon progresif, konsultasi gratis, relationship manager khusus.  
* Optimasi Proposal: Follow-up proaktif, perkuat value proposition, insentif terbatas waktu.  
* Referral Management: Apresiasi referrer kunci, identifikasi “Unknown Referrer”, survei kualitas referral.  
* Service Focus: Maksimalkan konversi di kategori Imigrasi & TKA, Family Law, dan Corporate.
